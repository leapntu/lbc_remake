Informed Consent

Basic mechanisms of learning in adulthood

Principal Investigator: Dr Luca ONNIS
Linguistics and Multilingual Studies
Nanyang Technological University

As part of this study, you will be asked to listen to a sequence of sounds, or to pay attention to a sequence of shapes. You will also be asked to read a list of simple sentences. You will also be asked recognize a list of literature authors, and to complete a “fill in the missing piece” visual task. Depending on your language you may be asked to fill out a brief language background questionnaire.

Although you may not receive direct benefit from this study, research in this area is of general benefit to society. The results will help us understand the processes involved in learning and using language. This knowledge will aid in improving effective teaching of a second language, or treating individuals with language disorders in the future.

There are no known discomforts or risks associated with participation in this study. The information you provide will remain confidential and will be used only for the purpose of the research. Your name will not be identified in any report of the results.

Your participation is voluntary and there is no penalty for refusing to participate. You can withdraw at any time you wish.
